\section{Research \texorpdfstring{\\}{i} Interests}

I am interested in promoting InSAR measurements accuracy and simplicity and understanding mechanics of slow moving landslides. My current focuses include:

\begin{itemize}
  \item Fast InSAR processing with GPU
  \item Landslides triggered by glacier retreat and GIA
  \item Deep learning for landslides detection and deformation mapping
\end{itemize}
